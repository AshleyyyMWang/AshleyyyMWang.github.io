Research Interests

My research interests lie in the fields of partial differential equations and graph theory. I work in both areas independently and also explore the connections between continuous and discrete frameworks.

One focus of my current work is developing graph alignment methods for complex networks.

I am also collaborating with Prof. Elizabeth Carlson, Prof. Franca Hoffmann, and Prof. Andrew Stuart on comparing data assimilation and parameter recovery algorithms, gaining insights into the distinctions between stochastic and deterministic approaches.
